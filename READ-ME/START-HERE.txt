Model of Water Scarcity Adaptation in Irrigated Agriculture

By Jason K. Hawes

This read me guides the user through the modeling framework "Model of Water Scarcity
Adaptation in Irrigated Agriculture."
This model was developed by Jason Hawes, Zhao Ma, Morey Burnham, and David Yu at
Purdue University and Idaho State University.
Written primarily in NetLogo, the program relies upon FAO's AquaCrop for some calculations.
